The 90-day pilot.

You don't commit long-term until it works. Here's how the first 90 days look — no contracts, no setup fees, fixed monthly rate that stops if you stop.

1
Week 1 — We watch your shop
We observe the estimating software, the emails, the slips. We identify which agent to start with for your specific operation.
2
Week 2 — We wire it up
We connect to your estimating software's data feed and your email. You don't install anything. Your staff keeps using the tools they already use.
3
Weeks 3–4 — Shadow mode
The agent runs but sends suggestions to your estimator instead of acting. You review every recommendation. You approve what's right. We tune what's wrong.
4
Week 5 onward — Live
The agent acts autonomously. You see every action in a weekly report. If any month the value isn't there, we refund that month and we both walk.

Notes on the math

Gross monthly recovery of $36,753 is a modeled average. At the Stack tier ($1,750/mo), net monthly leverage is $35,003. At the Operator tier ($3,200/mo), net is $33,553. Either way you're several multiples over breakeven in the first month.

Per-EV figure ($280) is additive — it kicks in proportional to your EV mix. A shop seeing 12 EVs per month adds $3,360.

Source numbers for agent-by-agent math come from the research report in the project files. Your shop's actual numbers will be different. We run the math on your numbers, not ours, during the Week 1 observation.
