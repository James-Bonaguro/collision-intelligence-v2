The promise

Three things this fixes.

Not "AI transformation." Not "digital strategy." Three specific, measurable things that happen in your shop every week that cost you real money — handled without anyone on your staff learning a new tool.

01
You stop tearing down cars that will total.
The agent flags likely total-loss jobs before the tech touches the vehicle. It locks in teardown compensation so you get paid even when the insurer writes it off.
02
You stop leaving money on every estimate.
The agent reads your draft estimate, compares it against the official labor time rules, and adds the lines your estimator missed — with citations the adjuster can't reject.
03
Your front office stops drowning in portals.
When a repair runs long, the agent extends the rental, notifies the insurer, and texts the customer — all of it, automatically, the moment it happens.
